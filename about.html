Welcome to BYD, a leading global electric vehicle manufacturer dedicated to bringing sustainable transportation to people everywhere.

Environment friendly

At BYD, we believe that the future of transportation is electric. That's why we are dedicated to developing and manufacturing advanced electric vehicles that provide our customers with clean, efficient, and sustainable transportation options.

Our begginings

Founded in 1995, BYD has been at the forefront of innovation in the automotive industry for over two decades. Our mission is to create a greener and better world by providing high-quality and affordable electric vehicles to people around the world.

Wide spectrum of vehicles

We offer a wide range of electric vehicles, from luxury sedans to budget-friendly SUVs, to meet the needs of drivers everywhere. Our vehicles are powered by our cutting-edge battery technology, which provides impressive driving range and fast charging capabilities.

Make a difference

At BYD, we are committed to making a positive impact on the world. From reducing emissions to improving energy efficiency, our vehicles are designed with the environment in mind. We are dedicated to providing our customers with sustainable transportation options that make a difference.

We are everywhere

With our growing network of dealerships and service centers, we are making it easier than ever for people everywhere to make the switch to electric vehicles. Whether you're looking for a luxury electric sedan, a budget-friendly electric SUV, or anything in between, BYD has a vehicle that's right for you.

The future

We believe that electric vehicles are the future of transportation and are dedicated to making the transition to electric vehicles as seamless and accessible as possible. Our cutting-edge technology, premium materials, and advanced features provide our customers with a driving experience that is both sustainable and enjoyable.

Join us!

Join the electric revolution today and start driving towards a sustainable future with BYD. We look forward to providing you with a high-quality and sustainable transportation solution that you'll love.

Thank you!

Thank you again for choosing BYD, and we look forward to being a part of your journey.
